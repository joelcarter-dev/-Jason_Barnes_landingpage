Hutel - Lake Grace

Hutel Lake Grace and The Lochinvar flats have been in Private ownership since 1857, Currently held in trust by the Barnes family of Canterbury.


Centrally located Ka Tiritiri O Te Mona, the Southern Alps of Canterbury New Zealand can be easily accessed by air, on foot or by vehicle via Mount White station.


Bordered by the scenic Poulter and Dampier Ranges and surrounded by Department of Conservation Open Hunting Areas, this is a quietly remote part of the country for many activities including Huntina, fishing, biking, tramping, wild photography. Also with the clearest of skies and lack of light pollution, astronomy and astrophotography !
